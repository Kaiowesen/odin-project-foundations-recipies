Dutch pancake

This breakfast pancake recipe is tasty and super fluffy using! It uses only three ingridients

Ingridients

  • 1/2 cups flour
  • 2/3 cups milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon clarified butter

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 250 degrees
  2. Mix all the ingrdients together in a blender, or by hand, until smooth
  3. Heat cast iron skillet with clarified butter, when pan is smoking pour the pancake batter in the skillet and transfer directly to hot oven
  4. Bake in oven for 20 to 25 minutes. The pancake is finished when the crust is golden brown and the pancake rises above the edge of the skillet
  5. Serve the pancake imidiately, with your favorite pancake topping.
