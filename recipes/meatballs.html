Grandma's meatball

This is a meatballs recipe from my grandmother. It is always served together with boiled potatoes and carrots

Ingridients

  • 500gr minced meat
  • 1 egg
  • 2 slices of bread
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons ground clove
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 litre beef stock

Steps

  1. Mix minced meat with eggs, milk, bread and spices. You can put the bread in the milk to soften it.
  2. Make meatballs to prefered size and fry in pan
  3. Make brown sacue: In a saucepan melt the butter and add flour when melted. Add beef stock little by litte until all liquid is added. Adjust with more liquid if needed
  4. Boil potatoes and carrots
  5. Serve meatballs with potatoes, carrots, brown sauce and ginger soda
