Milk soup

This is another recipe from my grandmother! It has only two ingridients and is suprisingly tasty

Ingridients

  • milk
  • macaroni

Steps

  1. Add milk and macaroni to saucepan.
  2. bring milk to a boil and cook until macaroni is tender. Make sure to stir continously to avoid milk burning
  3. When finished. Serve in a bowl.
